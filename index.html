NUFT: State of the Art Website

The platform (nufteg.com) provides a one-stop solution for accessing detailed information about Egyptian universities, admissions, scholarships, and more, helping students make informed decisions about their higher education journey.

Key Features:
1.Universities Guidebook: Detailed information on Egyptian universities, including faculties, fees, requirements, dormitories, transportation, and admission links.
2.Admissions Status: Real-time updates on universities that are still accepting applications.
3.Scholarships Search: Information on available scholarships and application details.
4.Certificates Equivalency: Tools to equate international certificates with Egyptian standards for university admission.
5.Grade-Based University Finder: Helps students identify universities and faculties where their grades meet the admission requirements.
6.Faculty Search: Locate universities offering specific faculties with comprehensive details on each institution.
7.Latest News: Up-to-date information on the educational sector.
8.Guides Easy Search: Quickly find a university’s full guide by searching with its name or acronym.
